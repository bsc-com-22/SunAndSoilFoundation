What We Do

Transforming Agriculture Through Innovation

We combine solar technology, climate-smart practices, and community empowerment to create sustainable change.

Solar-Powered Irrigation

Harnessing clean energy to enable year-round farming, reduce fuel dependency, and increase household food security.

  • Solar pump installation
  • Drip irrigation systems
  • Technical training & support

Climate-Smart Agriculture

Sustainable farming techniques that boost productivity while protecting the environment and building climate resilience.

  • Soil health management
  • Water-efficient practices
  • Crop diversification

Inclusive Livelihoods

Empowering women, youth, and vulnerable groups with skills, enterprise development, and sustainable financing.

  • Capacity building & mindset change
  • Women & youth enterprise
  • Sustainable financing models

The Context

Why This Work Matters

Malawi's agricultural sector faces unprecedented challenges that threaten food security and livelihoods.

80%

of Malawians depend on agriculture for survival

18.2%

face food insecurity annually (4 million+ people, 2025/26)

51%

of households remain below the poverty line

Key Challenges

  • Erratic rainfall and prolonged droughts
  • Rising costs of inputs and fuel
  • Limited access to credit and markets
  • Climate change impacts intensifying

Our Solution

  • Solar irrigation for year-round farming
  • Climate-smart techniques to boost yields
  • Training and capacity building programs
  • Community-led sustainable development
