Our Mission

Securing a Sustainable Food Future for Malawi

Climate change and erratic rainfall threaten the livelihoods of millions of Malawian farmers. We're changing that through solar-powered irrigation, climate-smart agriculture, and community empowerment. Together, we're building resilient farming communities that can thrive for generations to come.

Learn More About Our Work
The Context

Why This Work Matters

Malawi's agricultural sector faces unprecedented challenges that threaten food security and livelihoods.

80%

of Malawians depend on agriculture for survival

18.2%

face food insecurity annually (4 million+ people, 2025/26)

51%

of households remain below the poverty line

Key Challenges

  • Erratic rainfall and prolonged droughts
  • Rising costs of inputs and fuel
  • Limited access to credit and markets
  • Climate change impacts intensifying

Our Solution

  • Solar irrigation for year-round farming
  • Climate-smart techniques to boost yields
  • Training and capacity building programs
  • Community-led sustainable development
