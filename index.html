Who We Are

Rooted in Community. Driven by Impact.

Sun & Soil Foundation is a Malawian non-profit organization dedicated to transforming rural livelihoods through sustainable agriculture and clean energy solutions.

We believe the sun and the soil are Malawi’s greatest assets. By harnessing solar energy for irrigation and promoting climate-smart farming practices, we help smallholder farmers increase yields, improve food security, and build long-term economic resilience.

Our work is grounded in community partnerships, local leadership, and practical solutions that respond directly to the realities farmers face on the ground.

Read Our Story

The Context

Why This Work Matters

Malawi’s agricultural sector faces unprecedented challenges that threaten food security, rural livelihoods, and national development.

The Reality

80% of Malawians depend on agriculture for survival 18.2% face food insecurity annually (Over 4 million people) 51% of households remain below the poverty line

Key Challenges

  • Erratic rainfall and prolonged droughts
  • Rising costs of agricultural inputs and fuel
  • Limited access to credit, technology, and markets
  • Intensifying climate change impacts

Our Solution

  • Solar-powered irrigation for year-round farming
  • Climate-smart techniques to improve yields sustainably
  • Training and capacity building for farmers and cooperatives
  • Community-led development for long-term impact

Our Approach: By integrating clean energy, sustainable agriculture, and local capacity building, we help farming communities transition from subsistence to resilience—improving food security, incomes, and climate adaptability.
