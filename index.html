THE CHALLENGE

Why This Work Matters

Malawi's agricultural sector faces unprecedented challenges that threaten food security, rural livelihoods, and national development.

The Reality

80%

of Malawians depend on agriculture for survival

18.2%

More than 4 million people face annual food insecurity (2025/26)

51%

of households remain below the poverty line

Key Challenges

Erratic Rainfall

Prolonged droughts and unpredictable rainfall patterns threaten crop yields

Rising Costs

Increasing prices of agricultural inputs and fuel limit farming productivity

Limited Access

Farmers lack access to credit, modern technology, and profitable markets

Climate Change

Intensifying climate impacts create unprecedented agricultural challenges
