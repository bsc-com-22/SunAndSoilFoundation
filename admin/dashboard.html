Getting Started

Welcome to your Content Management System! Here's how to get started:

  1. Upload Media: Add images to your media library for use across the site
  2. Edit Pages: Update homepage content, about page, and other sections
  3. Add Team Members: Showcase your team with photos and bios
  4. Create Projects: Highlight your impact with project case studies
  5. Update Metrics: Keep your impact statistics current
  6. Publish: Review changes and publish to make them live
