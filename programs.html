Our Programs

We implement integrated solutions designed to address the root causes of poverty and food insecurity in rural Malawi.

Solar-Powered Irrigation

We install high-efficiency solar water pumps that allow farmers to irrigate their crops year-round, reducing reliance on erratic rainfall and diesel pumps.

KEY IMPACT AREAS

  • Reduced irrigation costs by 100%
  • Year-round crop production
  • Clean energy adoption

Climate-Smart Agriculture

We train farmers in conservation agriculture, agroforestry, and organic farming techniques to improve soil health and resilience against droughts and floods.

KEY IMPACT AREAS

  • Improved soil fertility
  • Increased crop yields
  • Biodiversity conservation

Capacity Building

We strengthen farmer cooperatives through training in financial literacy, governance, and market negotiation to ensure long-term sustainability.

KEY IMPACT AREAS

  • Stronger cooperatives
  • Better market prices
  • Financial inclusion

Women & Youth Empowerment

We prioritize women and youth in all our interventions, providing targeted support to close the gender gap in agriculture and create rural employment.

KEY IMPACT AREAS

  • Economic independence
  • Leadership roles
  • Youth employment
