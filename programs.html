Our Programs

Integrated Solutions for Sustainable Change

Our programs work together to address the interconnected challenges facing rural communities — from water access to skills development to market integration.

Solar-Powered Irrigation

Harnessing the sun to water the land

We install and maintain solar-powered water pumping systems that enable year-round farming, reducing dependence on unpredictable rainfall and creating climate resilience.

  • Installation of solar pumps and drip irrigation systems
  • Training on system operation and maintenance
  • Formation of water user associations
  • Technical support and monitoring
150+ systems installed across 12 districts

Climate-Smart Agriculture

Sustainable techniques for a changing climate

Our comprehensive training programs equip farmers with proven techniques to increase productivity while protecting the environment and adapting to climate change.

  • Soil health management and conservation
  • Integrated pest management
  • Crop diversification strategies
  • Water-efficient farming techniques
5,000+ farmers trained in CSA techniques

Capacity Building

Knowledge that transforms communities

We build the skills and organizational capacity of farmer groups, cooperatives, and community leaders to drive their own development and sustain impact beyond our direct involvement.

  • Financial literacy and savings groups
  • Leadership and governance training
  • Market linkages and value addition
  • Organizational development
200+ community groups strengthened

Women & Youth Empowerment

Investing in tomorrow's leaders

We prioritize women and young people in all our programs, providing targeted support to overcome barriers and create pathways to economic opportunity and leadership.

  • Women-focused agricultural training
  • Youth entrepreneurship programs
  • Leadership development for women
  • Access to finance and inputs
60% of beneficiaries are women and youth
