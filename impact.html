Our Impact

Measuring change, celebrating progress, and learning for the future.

IMPACT OVERVIEW

Real People. Real Change.

Impact is at the heart of everything we do. We measure success not just in numbers, but in the lives transformed, communities strengthened, and futures secured.

Since our inception, Sun & Soil Foundation has been building a foundation for sustainable rural development in Malawi, supporting smallholder farmers to move from climate-vulnerable subsistence farming toward productive, market-oriented, and resilient agricultural systems.
