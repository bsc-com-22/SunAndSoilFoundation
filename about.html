About Us

Building a sustainable, food-secure future for Malawi through innovation and community partnership

Our Story

Born From a Vision of Self-Sufficiency

Our work combines renewable energy deployment, hands-on training, and inclusive enterprise development, aligned with Malawi Vision 2063, the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), and ESG principles.

To date, Sun & Soil Foundation has implemented solar-powered irrigation installations and capacity-building programmes across Malawi, supporting 10 smallholder farmers and training 85 women, youth, and farmers, enabling year-round production, reduced fuel dependency, and improved household food security.

Over the next five years, Sun & Soil Foundation aims to reach 50,000 farmers nationwide.

Our Direction

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A Malawi where every community is food secure, climate-resilient, economically empowered, and powered by clean energy.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable households through solar-powered agriculture, climate-smart farming, and inclusive livelihood programmes.
