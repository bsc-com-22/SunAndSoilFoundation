About Us

Transforming Lives Through Sustainable Agriculture

Sun & Soil Foundation is a Malawian non-profit organization dedicated to building food security and climate resilience through solar-powered irrigation and sustainable farming practices.

Our Story

Born From a Vision of Self-Sufficiency

Sun & Soil Foundation was established with a clear mission: to harness the abundant solar resources of Malawi to transform smallholder farming and rural livelihoods.

We recognized that while Malawi has vast agricultural potential, many farmers face recurring challenges — unreliable rainfall, limited access to irrigation, and lack of training in modern, sustainable techniques.

By combining solar technology with climate-smart agriculture and community capacity building, we're creating a new model for rural development that puts farmers at the center of their own transformation.

Our Vision

A Malawi where every rural community has access to clean energy, sustainable water resources, and the knowledge to build food security and prosperity for generations to come.

Our Mission

To empower smallholder farmers and rural communities through solar-powered irrigation, climate-smart agriculture training, and sustainable livelihood programs that build resilience and self-sufficiency.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

These principles shape every program we design and every partnership we build.

Community First

We listen, learn, and work alongside the communities we serve, respecting local knowledge and traditions.

Integrity

Transparency and accountability guide everything we do, from project implementation to donor reporting.

Sustainability

Our programs are designed for long-term impact, building local capacity and ownership.

Inclusion

We prioritize women, youth, and marginalized groups in all our programs and decision-making.
