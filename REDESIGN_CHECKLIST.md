## üí° Tips for Success

1. **Test Early, Test Often**: Don't wait until the end to test
2. **Get Feedback**: Show to users and stakeholders regularly
3. **Use Real Content**: Replace placeholders as soon as possible
4. **Stay Consistent**: Use the design system throughout
5. **Prioritize Mobile**: Most users will visit on phones
6. **Monitor Performance**: Keep the site fast
7. **Update Regularly**: Keep news and content fresh
8. **Train Your Team**: Ensure everyone can use the CMS
