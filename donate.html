Invest in Malawi's Future

Your contribution directly empowers farmers with solar irrigation, climate-smart training, and the tools to build sustainable livelihoods.

Why Your Support Matters

In Malawi, smallholder farmers face increasing challenges from climate change. Erratic rainfall and droughts threaten food security for millions. By supporting Sun & Soil Foundation, you are providing a lifeline.

Solar Irrigation

$500 provides a complete solar pump kit for a farming family, enabling year-round cultivation.

Seeds & Inputs

$50 supplies drought-resistant seeds and organic fertilizer for one season.

Farmer Training

$100 trains a lead farmer in climate-smart agriculture techniques to share with their community.
